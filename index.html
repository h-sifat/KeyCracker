A simple application to improve your individual keypress. Click on any item (letter, timer (Enter as minutes, e.g., 1), targetMilliSeconds) then enter there value in the input field and hit enter. The top right indicator is your keypress time in ms. Double click the stop button to reset the timer. Type: s=0 or s=1 in the input box and hit Enter to mute/unmute
